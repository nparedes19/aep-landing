About Us

Founded in 2020 by Colombian designer Alcibiades Estupiñán, AEP Design Solutions grew from a vision rooted in home design, interiors, and landscapes.

We believe every space has the power to inspire. Our work goes beyond function—we create spaces that are authentic, welcoming, and aligned with the way people live and connect. Each project is a journey of collaboration and discovery, where design becomes a tool to transform everyday moments into meaningful experiences. From residential updates to commercial projects, our focus is always on creating spaces that feel natural, timeless, and true to their context.

At AEP Design Solutions, our commitment is to design with intention and care. By blending creativity with functionality, we strive to deliver spaces that not only enhance everyday life but also leave a lasting sense of comfort, harmony, and inspiration.
