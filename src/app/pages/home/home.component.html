Solution

AEP Architecture is a Design Studio dedicated to creating spaces that feel authentic, functional and inspiring, where form, function and nature coexist in harmony.

We offer personalized solutions in Architecture, Interior Design and Landscape Design, thoughtfully integrating every element to reflect the character of each place. From small residential renovations to large-scale commercial developments, our team provides full-service support from concept to installation.

About us

AEP Architecture was founded in 2020 by Alcibiades Estupiñán, a Colombian architect and graduate of Universidad San Buenaventura in Cali, Colombia. What began as a personal vision has grown into a collaborative design studio dedicated to creating thoughtful, sustainable spaces for everyday life. The studio is built on a simple belief: that intentional design has the power to improve lives, shape meaningful experiences, and deepen our connection to the spaces we inhabit
