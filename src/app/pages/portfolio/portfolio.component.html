Austin, Texas

110 Laurel

Austin, Texas

This custom landscape project brings a quiet corner to life using Texas native plants carefully selected for a narrow bed along a wooden fence. The design blends seasonal color, textural variety, and pollinator-friendly blooms like Purple Coneflower and Gregg’s Mistflower with soft grasses and vines to create vertical interest and movement. A mix of perennials, ground covers, and climbing vines were arranged to provide visual layers offering structure, softness, and seasonal rhythm. All plants were chosen for their resilience, low-maintenance needs, and ecological value, encouraging biodiversity while remaining water-wise. A small artistic touch a rustic vine basket anchors the front section, adding charm and personality to the planting. This is a great example of how even a narrow side yard can become a lush, welcoming moment with intentional design.

Mi Momento – Nail Spa

Jamundí, Colombia

Located in Jamundí, Colombia, Mi Momento is a commercial nail spa project where I led both the architectural and interior design. The concept was built around creating a calming, elegant, and contemporary environment tailored to enhance the client experience. Natural textures, warm wood finishes, and carefully integrated lighting define the atmosphere, while the layout optimizes flow, comfort, and functionality for both customers and staff. Every element from the custom pedicure stations to the shelving and lighting design was crafted to reflect the brand’s identity as a modern, welcoming space for self-care and relaxation.

Collaborative Projects

Río Élite Residential Tower

Cali, Colombia

Río Élite is a luxury residential tower located in the exclusive Santa Mónica neighborhood of Cali, Colombia. Designed in collaboration with Dussan Arquitectos, this multifamily project redefines upscale living with sweeping city views, exceptional natural light, and a privileged location near the Bataclán nature reserve. The building features 18 spacious apartments, each with generous layouts, three parking spaces, private storage, and access to high-end amenities including a gym, pool, BBQ area, and entertainment rooms. We collaborated on the architectural design and contributed to key spatial and aesthetic decisions, helping shape an elegant, contemporary tower that offers both urban sophistication and a close connection to nature.
