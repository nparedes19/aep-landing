611 Residential Design

Austin, Texas

This custom designed single-family home in Austin, Texas, is a personal architectural and interior design project that blends contemporary form with warm natural textures. I was responsible for the complete architectural concept and interior layout, crafting a modern yet inviting space that responds to its wooded surroundings. Mi Momento – Nail Spa

Jamundí, Colombia

Located in Jamundí, Colombia, Mi Momento is a commercial nail spa project where I led both the architectural and interior design. The concept was built around creating a calming, elegant, and contemporary environment tailored to enhance the client experience. Natural textures, warm wood finishes, and carefully integrated lighting define the atmosphere, while the layout optimizes flow, comfort, and functionality for both customers and staff. Every element from the custom pedicure stations to the shelving and lighting design was crafted to reflect the brand’s identity as a modern, welcoming space for self-care and relaxation.

Río Élite Residential Tower

Cali, Colombia

Río Élite is a luxury residential tower in the upscale Santa Mónica area of Cali, developed in partnership with Dussan Arquitectos. The building houses 18 spacious, light filled apartments each including three parking spaces and a private storage room and boasts sweeping city views and close proximity to the Bataclán nature reserve. Residents enjoy high-end amenities such as a gym, Turkish bath, pool, BBQ area, and lounge spaces. I contributed to the architectural design, shaping key spatial and aesthetic decisions to deliver a contemporary, elegant tower that blends urban sophistication with natural connection.
